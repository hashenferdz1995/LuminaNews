Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. At LuminaNews, we use cookies to provide personalized content and analyze our traffic.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly.

Analytical Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with the website.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): Used to deliver personalized advertisements to you based on your interests.

3. Google AdSense & DoubleClick Cookie

As a third-party vendor, Google uses cookies to serve ads on LuminaNews. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

You can manage your ad preferences through your Google Ad Settings.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you can also manage your consent preferences through the consent popup (CMP) that appears when you first visit our site.
